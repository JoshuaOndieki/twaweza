Company Profile

We are a Kenyan company focused on promoting tourism in Kenya. Our aim is to ensure that we invest in advertising our country's rich heritage to the outside world. We are also ensuring that tourists visiting our country do not regret making the visit due to various reasons:

  • Unneccesary spending of tourist's money for simple favours such as directions. THis is a very common norm that people have adapted to rip off tourists.
  • Less fullfilment for tourists due to lack of knowledge of where one can visit.
  • Limits due to unplanned expenditures.
  • Time wasted finding tour guides.

How it Works

Our business model is a simple one. All you need is to book for your vacation as a package.

Further Explanation

  • Tourists can navigate through our website to find out about tourists destinations they could like to visit.
  • You can add as many tourists destinations as you like to the cart.
  • Once your bucketlist is enough, you can checkout and pay for the full vacation package.
  • NOTE: The prices shown are inclusive of minor expenditure such as small bills and emergencies.

    • TIP: You can do research and find out other tourists destinations not included in our list. We may be able to accomodate them in your package.
